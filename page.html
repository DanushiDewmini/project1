JAPAN TRADITIONAL FOODS

japan food

Japanese food is known for its unique flavors, beautiful presentation, and healthy ingredients. One of the most popular dishes is sushi, which consists of vinegared rice paired with a variety of fish, vegetables, and sometimes fruits. Sushi can be eaten in different forms, such as nigiri, which is a slice of fish on a small mound of rice, or maki, which is rolled sushi wrapped in seaweed. Another beloved dish is ramen, a comforting noodle soup that comes in many regional varieties. Each type of ramen has its own broth, which can be rich and meaty or light and refreshing. Japanese cuisine also features tempura, lightly battered and fried vegetables or seafood, which is crunchy on the outside and tender on the inside.
